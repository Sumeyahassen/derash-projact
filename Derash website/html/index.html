DERAH FEATURES

ANBESA BUS

Derash helps passengers easily track Anbessa city buses in real time, check routes and schedules, and use the Derash Card for cashless payments. It improves boarding safety, reduces waiting time, and protects users from theft by showing trusted bus information.

Read More
SHEGER BUS

With Sheger buses, Derash offers a digital booking system, live location tracking, and seat availability updates. Passengers can scan their Derash Card to enter, receive alerts about upcoming stops, and report safety concerns directly from the app.

Read More
PUBLIC BIS

For traditional public minibuses, Derash connects passengers to available nearby routes and stops using a GPS map. It helps users identify safe, registered minibus drivers and lets them pay using the Derash Card or mobile wallet without needing cash.
